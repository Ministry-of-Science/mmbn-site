Megaman Battle Network

This site is a collection of MMBN-related projects ranging from real-world attempts to bring the world of Mega Man Battle Network to reality, to games, to community.

If you know of a project that would be great for this site, let me know via Twitter (@NetOpWibby) or by email (netopwibby@thenetwork.email).

  • /r/BattleNetwork

    The series may be dead, but that doesn't mean the fans are. The MMBN community is thriving, and more people are joining weekly. Quite amazing, really.

    - Visit the subreddit

  • chiplibrary

    Chris Santiago is working on a BattleChip library and would love to have help on!

    - Visit chiplibrary

  • EndCycle

    EndCycle is an Action RPG, with a skill based combat system and a focus on letting the player develop their own combat style. It is inspired by MMBN and developed by 12B3Games. It now has online multiplayer!

    - Visit the Official Site.

  • Mega Man Battle Network (Series) Chip List

    This chip list is maintained by Tterraj42 and cheeseandcereal. Some game IDs for BN6 and a lot of the Japanese names are missing, but it's mostly complete.

    - Visit the Google Doc

  • Ministry of Science

    Also known as SciLab, this is where NetOperator Wibby is working to bring MMBN to the real-world (when he has time away from real-world duties).

    - Join the Slack group and visit the project repos

  • MMBN speedruns

    TeamBN does speedrun marathons on their Twitch channel every now and then. Their Discord group is for general silliness and fun involving plenty of memes and MMBN stuff.

    - Visit TeamBN on Discord, Twitch, Twitter, and YouTube.

  • MMBN3D

    MMBN3D is an awesome NetBattling fan-game designed and coded by HeatPhoenix.

    - Download MMBN3D and visit the dev blog

  • MMBN Chrono X

    Chrono X is the unofficial 7th entry in the MMBN series. The game is very much a work-in-progress, but the dedicated creators push out regular updates.

    - Download the demo and visit the forum

  • mmbno

    Brandon Evans is working on a NetBattle simulator.

    - Visit mmbno

  • MMBN Project Genesis

    MMBN Project Genesis is a fan game of the MegaMan Battle Network games. Made by people who didn't like the ending of 6 and thought the game should continue after CX.

    - Visit the site to download the early access demo (Windows only).

  • One Step From Eden

    One Step From Eden is a realtime tile-battle deck-building rogue-lite. For a game created by just one guy, it's damn good-looking. The battle animations are fluid as heck.

    - Visit the site to check out the progress and read the devblog.

  • Shanghai.EXE Genso Network

    This fangame is a cross between Touhou and MMBN. Even though the game is in Japanese, there is an English guide you can follow.

    - Visit the translation guide. There's a link at the top of the page for downloading the game.

  • Sprites INC

    These people have been around for years, providing quality sprites rips from the Battle Network games.

    - Visit the site

  • Tri Wing

    Tri Wing is an online real-time action and grid based card game for PC and mobile. It is inspired by MMBN and developed by Vincent Veak.

    - Visit the Kickstarter!
